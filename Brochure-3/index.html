About the Project

FLOW PARK Residence

Flow Park Residence is a landmark compound set in the heart of Obour City. Built across elevated terrain with 35 residential buildings and 920 premium units, it delivers a living experience defined by greenery, clean architecture, and a 1.5 km facade along the city's main artery.

Every home is designed to maximise natural light, airflow, and views — with green spines running through each block and a full suite of amenities supporting every aspect of modern life.
